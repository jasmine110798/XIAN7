Convenient transportation

Metro, buses, shared bicycles, travel lines, and taxis are one of the most time -saving transportation methods in Xi'an. 1.Subway There are currently 4 subway lines in Xi'an: Line 1/2/3/4. You can buy a ticket yourself or you can use Alipay to scan the code, which is very convenient. 2. Bus As a mature tourist city, there are many buses in Xi'an, which is also very convenient. If you need to choose a bus, you can search on the Gaode map or Baidu map. It is still very accurate, but everyone should not take the opposite direction. 3.Shared bicycle Xi'an is located in the plain, which is very suitable for cycling. Many people on the street ride, and renting is also very convenient. 4.tourist line he Shaanxi History Museum, Datang Furong Garden, etc., the fare is 1 yuan, and they are all getting off at the main entrance of the attraction, which is more convenient.
